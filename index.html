Launch Content Kit

Fourteen ready to build posts — seven to tease the launch, seven to carry it after the doors open. Every slide is written in the club voice and colored in the brand palette. Copy the text straight into Canva and drop your photos in.

Canva quick start

Build two blank designs in Canva: a 1080 × 1350 px portrait (4:5) for carousels and a 1080 × 1920 px story. Set these brand pieces once, save as a Brand Kit, and reuse for every post below.

Brand colors (paste the hex)

Ink · #1A1A1A
Bone · #F4EFE6
Terracotta · #A8572C
Cypress · #3D5447
Linen · #C9C3B5

Type

  • Headlines: a serif — Georgia, or in Canva use Playfair Display / DM Serif Display. Regular weight, never bold.
  • Eyebrow / labels: a clean sans in ALL CAPS with wide letter spacing (220) — Helvetica or Archivo.

Your logo files

  • Upload from brand-dropzone › The Headshot Club Logo › PNG (or the SVG folder for crisp scaling).
  • On dark / ink slides use the light logo. On bone slides use the dark logo.
  • Keep the logo small and in a bottom corner. Let the type lead.

The signature move

  • The thin corner brackets framing each slide (like a viewfinder) are pulled from your logo. In Canva: add four thin L shaped lines in the corners, terracotta or bone. This single detail makes a plain text slide read as "the club."
  • Generous margins. One idea per slide. Let it breathe.

A simple posting rhythm

No fixed dates needed. A calm, twice a week cadence keeps the feed editorial rather than noisy.

Before launch

  • Wk 1: Post 01 (teaser) → Post 02 (manifesto)
  • Wk 2: Post 03 (who it is for) → Post 04 (the collective)
  • Wk 3: Post 05 (what works) → Post 06 (doors open)
  • Launch week: Post 07 (first access), then go live

After launch

  • Day 1: Post 08 (we are open) — pin it
  • Wk 1: Post 09 (how it works) → Post 10 (sessions)
  • Wk 2: Post 11 (the difference) → Post 12 (in their words)
  • Wk 3: Post 13 (make it count) → Post 14 (inside a session)
